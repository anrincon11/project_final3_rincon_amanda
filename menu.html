Appetizers

Roasted Brussels Sprouts with Balsamic Vinegar and Honey ~ 8

These Brussels sprouts are roasted in a high temperature oven until golden brown and crisp, then tossed with a touch of balsamic vinegar and honey and topped with bacon pieces.

Coquilles Saint-Jacques ~ 10

Scallops are poached in white wine, placed atop a purée of mushrooms in a scallop shell, covered with a sauce made of the scallop poaching liquid, and gratinéed under a broiler.

Baked Camembert ~ 6

A lovely gooey baked Camembert with loads of crusty bread for dipping and nutty cranberry crumbles.

Entrees

Confit de Canard ~ 23

Duck leg salted with herbs and slowly cooked in its own rendered fat. Served with potatoes roasted in duck fat.

Ratatouille ~ 18

Eggplant and zucchini simmered with tomatoes, caramelized onions and garlic until tender and juicy.

Poulet Basquaise ~ 15

Chicken stew made with tomatoes and red bell peppers.

Roasted Chicken and Garlic ~ 12

Roasted chicken with garlic, seasoned with thyme and served with potatoes.

Boeuf Bourguignon ~ 15

A traditional French stew prepared with beef braised in red wine.

Desserts

Berry Filled Crepes ~ 6

Delectable thin, French pancakes filled with your choice of strawberry, raspberry, or blueberry filling, topped with fresh fruit.

French Apple Tart ~ 5

Pre-baked sweet pastry crust layered with lightly sweetened apple sauce, topped with artfully arranged apples slices.

Chocolate Profiteroles ~ 5

French choux pastry ball filled with a sweet and moist filling of whipped cream, custard, pastry cream, garnished with chocolate sauce.

Drinks

French 75 ~ 8

A cocktail of gin, brandy, and champagne.

The French Blondie ~ 10

St. Germain elderflower liqueur, gin, and White Lillet are mixed with grapefruit juice in an ethereal citrus drink.

The French Connection ~ 10

Inspired by Woody Allen's Midnight in Paris, this cocktail combines cognac with fresh raspberries and sparkling white wine.

Boneshaker Zinfandel ~ 12/48

Brick red and deep purple in the glass, with enticing aromatics of dried plumbs, black cherry jam and a hint of vanilla incense. One sip unveils falvors of blackberry, dark chocolate and licorice. Smooth on the finish with bracing tannins and lingering notes of blueberry and spice.

True Myth Cabernet Sauvignon ~ 12/48

This rich yet smooth Cabernet Sauvignon is loaded with elegant aromas of blackberry, blueberry, pepper and cassis which lead to flavors of dark red fruits with hints of cedar spice, cocoa powder and caramelized oak. These persistent flavors create a long lingering finish.

Ca' Montini Trentino Pinot Grigio ~ 11/44

Straw yellow in color, the wine shows fresh apple peach and citrus flavors offset by hints of almonds and hazelnut. The texture is precisely balanced between ripe fruit and acidity, ending on a crisp, vibrant note.
